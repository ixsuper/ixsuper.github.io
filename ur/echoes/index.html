Built on Apple’s Latest

Echoes is designed from the ground up for iOS 26, using SwiftUI, Liquid Glass, CoreML, MapKit, and AVFoundation. Every interaction is tuned for 120 Hz ProMotion, CoreHaptics, and the new system-wide material layer.

  • Swift 6 strict concurrency throughout.
  • CoreML for on-device scene, emotion, and audio classification.
  • MapKit 3D with custom terrain and selective POI filtering.
  • AVFoundation capture pipeline with spatial audio support.
  • WeatherKit for a lightweight snapshot of conditions at capture time.
