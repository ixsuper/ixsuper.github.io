Color Number Match Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ziyad Alsuhaymi ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the Color Number Match mobile application (the "App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We prioritize your privacy. Most of the data generated by Color Number Match is stored locally on your device.

A. Data Used to Track You

The following data may be used to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies:

  • Device ID: Your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) is collected by our advertising partner to serve relevant ads.
  • Advertising Data: Information about ads you have viewed or interacted with.

B. Data Not Linked to You

We may collect the following data for analytics and app improvement, which is not linked to your personal identity:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (e.g., levels played, features used) to help us improve the user experience.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance data to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to help us operate the App:

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display interstitial and rewarded advertisements (including an optional "watch ad for +3 extra moves" offer). AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and usage data. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking). Read Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center: Used for leaderboards and achievements. Managed by Apple. Read Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Used to process in-app purchases. All transactions are handled securely by Apple.
  • Apple Push Notifications: Used to deliver opt-in reminders (daily challenge, streak, lives full, and inactivity). Notifications are scheduled locally on your device and can be disabled at any time in iOS Settings > Notifications > Color Number Match.

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases including coins, power-ups, ad removal, and unlimited lives. All purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. You can manage or cancel purchases through your Apple ID settings.

4. Your Choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on your iOS device.
  • Remove ads entirely: Purchase the "Remove Ads" option within the app.
  • Disable notifications: Go to Settings > Notifications > Color Number Match to turn off any or all reminders.
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at the email below to request deletion of any data associated with your device.

5. Data Security

  • Local Storage: Your game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage.
  • No Account Required: The app does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to play.

6. Children's Privacy

Color Number Match is rated 4+ and is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The ads displayed are configured for a general audience.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date.
