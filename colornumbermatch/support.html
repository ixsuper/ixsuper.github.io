Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Purchases > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings > Language & Notifications. Color Number Match supports 24 languages including Arabic with full right-to-left layout support.

I lost my progress. Can I recover it?

Game data is stored locally on your device. If you need to start fresh, go to Settings > Data & Privacy to reset your data. Unfortunately, deleted progress cannot be recovered.

How do special tiles work?

Bomb tiles clear a 3x3 area around them when matched. Rainbow tiles can match with any color or number. Frozen tiles are locked and require multiple matches to break free.

How do I earn more coins?

You can earn coins by winning levels, collecting daily rewards, maintaining a daily streak, watching rewarded ads, or purchasing coins in the shop.

Does the app collect my data?

No. All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.
