GameShelf Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ziyad Alsuhaymi ("we," "us," or "our") handles information in the GameShelf mobile application (the "App"). GameShelf is built for privacy: no account is required, no personal profile is created, and AI guides are generated entirely on your device.

1. What GameShelf Stores

Your tracked games, followed games, reminder preferences, launch-tab choice, and saved guides live on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you enable iCloud sync, a copy is stored in your private iCloud container via CloudKit — accessible only to you across your own Apple devices. We never see or receive this data.

2. What GameShelf Does Not Collect

  • No account registration, email, or password.
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA) and no tracking across apps or websites.
  • No analytics SDK that links a user identity.
  • No third-party ads anywhere in the app.

3. On-Device AI Guides

Game guides, tips, and walkthroughs are generated by Apple Foundation Models, which run entirely on your device. Your prompts, the games you look up, and the generated text never leave your phone and are never sent to us or any third party. Guides require iOS 26 or later on a device with Apple Intelligence enabled.

4. Third-Party Data Sources

GameShelf fetches public, non-personalized game data from the following services. These requests contain only the query needed to return data (e.g., a game title or IGDB ID) — never your identity.

  • IGDB (Internet Game Database): Game metadata, cover art, release dates, platforms, genres, ratings, and time-to-beat durations. IGDB Privacy.
  • Twitch: Live stream lookups for "who is streaming this game right now." Twitch Privacy Notice.
  • CheapShark: Public price and deal information across PC storefronts (Steam, GOG, Epic, Humble, Fanatical, and others). CheapShark API.
  • Steam Web API: Public current-player counts for released PC games.

5. Notifications

Launch reminders, release-eve alerts, and storage-nudge notifications are scheduled locally by iOS. They fire on your device based on release dates stored on your device. No reminder data is sent to any server.

6. Calendar Integration

If you add a release to your calendar, the event is written directly to the Apple Calendar database on your device via EventKit, with your explicit permission. We do not see or store calendar events.

7. In-App Purchases

GameShelf Pro is an optional subscription that unlocks unlimited tracked games, the Global Launch Hub, reminder preset customization, calendar export, and Year in Review. Purchases are processed by Apple StoreKit. We receive only an anonymous receipt confirming entitlement status — no name, email, or payment details.

8. Crash and Diagnostics

If you have "Share With App Developers" enabled in iOS Settings, Apple may share anonymized crash logs and performance metrics with us. These contain no personal information and are used only to fix bugs.

9. Your Choices

  • Disable iCloud sync: turn off iCloud for GameShelf in iOS Settings to keep all data local.
  • Revoke notification permission: Settings > Notifications > GameShelf.
  • Revoke calendar permission: Settings > Privacy & Security > Calendars.
  • Delete everything: remove GameShelf from your device; iCloud data can be deleted in Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Account Storage.

10. Children's Privacy

GameShelf is rated 12+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will appear on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
