What's Inside

  • Discover — browse upcoming and released games by platform, filter by PlayStation / Xbox / Nintendo / PC.
  • Tracking — follow your list across devices via iCloud, with Spotlight, Widgets, and Live Activities.
  • Pulse — a curated feed of trending games on Twitch right now.
  • Guides — on-device AI walkthroughs that never leave your phone.
  • Settings — launch-tab picker, reminder presets, subscription badges, and a clean Pro upgrade flow.

Privacy First

Upcomin' stores your list locally with SwiftData and optionally syncs across your own Apple devices via CloudKit. AI guides run on-device using Apple Foundation Models — no prompts ever leave your phone. No account required, no third-party tracking, no ads.
